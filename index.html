Discover the Charm of Gouda

Explore history, cheese, and the beautiful surroundings.

The Historic Heart of Gouda

Gouda, with its medieval charm, is centered around the iconic Stadhuis. Founded in the 11th century, it boasts the Sint-Janskerk with its magnificent stained glass. The Gouda Museum showcases the city's rich history and art. The city's canals and historic buildings offer a glimpse into its past.

The city's strategic location along the Gouwe river contributed to its prosperity, making it a key trading hub. Today, visitors can explore the many historic sites, including the Sint-Janskerk, famous for its stunning stained glass windows, and the Gouda Museum, which showcases the city's rich history and art.

Gouda Cheese: A Dutch Treasure

Gouda cheese, a worldwide favorite, originates from this region. The traditional cheese market in Markt is a must-see. Learn about the cheese-making process and sample various ages of Gouda, from young to aged.

The cheese is made from cow's milk and comes in various ages, each with a distinct flavor profile. From young Gouda, which is mild and creamy, to aged Gouda, which is rich and nutty, there's a variety to suit every palate. The cheese is traditionally made by farmers in the surrounding region, and the process involves curdling the milk, pressing the curds into molds, and aging the cheese for varying periods.

Gouda Candles: A Warm Tradition

Gouda is also known for its high-quality candles. The annual Gouda Candle Night in December is a magical event.

The Gouda Market: A Lively Experience

The Markt offers local products like cheese and stroopwafels. Held every Wednesday and Saturday.

Reeuwijk and the Reeuwijkse Plassen

Just a short distance from Gouda, Reeuwijk is known for its beautiful lakes, the Reeuwijkse Plassen. This area is perfect for boating, swimming, and nature walks. Explore the serene landscapes and enjoy the outdoor activities. Reeuwijk offers a peaceful retreat with its scenic waterways and green surroundings, ideal for relaxation and recreation.

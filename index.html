Welcome to Gouda

Savor the Legacy of a Historic Cheese City

A Tapestry of History

Gouda’s medieval charm unfolds around the iconic Stadhuis, a masterpiece of Gothic architecture. Founded in the 11th century, the city’s Sint-Janskerk boasts breathtaking stained glass, while its canals whisper tales of a prosperous past as a trading hub along the Gouwe river.

The Soul of Gouda Cheese

From creamy young wheels to nutty aged treasures, Gouda cheese is a Dutch icon born here. Witness the vibrant cheese market in Markt and delve into centuries-old traditions of craftsmanship.

Candlelight & Tradition

Gouda’s candle-making heritage glows brightest during the annual Candle Night, when the city transforms into a warm, flickering wonderland each December.

The Bustling Markt

The historic Markt square comes alive with the cheese market, a tradition steeped in Gouda’s identity. Traders, farmers, and visitors mingle amidst golden wheels of cheese.

Reeuwijkse Plassen

Just beyond the city, the serene Reeuwijkse Plassen lakes offer a peaceful retreat, framed by nature’s beauty.
